# Reading

This is a list of books and other written works that I either would like to read or have read and liked.

## Favourites

### The Witcher

_The Witcher_, by Andrzej Sapkowski, is a series of 2 short story collections, 5 novels, and 2 standalone novels, all of which I have read. I find the writing to be excellent in many ways, including the themes and messages that it deals with.

### Various Works of Franz Kafka

Out of Franz Kafka's works, I have read (English translations of):

- Before the Law
- The Trial
- The Metamorphosis

I like all three, but my favourite would be The Metamorphosis.
