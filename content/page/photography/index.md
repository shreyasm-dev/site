# Photography

The subjects of most of my photos are birds, but there are also some small mammals, snakes, natural scenes, and insects. Most were captured on an old Nikon D5100 DSLR, though there are some captured on newer mirrorless cameras that I rented.

The metadata that is displayed is automatically extracted from the images. One of the cameras I rented had a defective clock (namely the Sony α7R IV), and I haven't bothered to edit the metadata manually, so the images purportedly taken in 2019 are actually from 2025.

Also see my [favourite photos](/photography/favourites).
